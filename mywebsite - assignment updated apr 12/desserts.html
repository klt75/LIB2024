Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup cold coffee
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

    • Optional glaze

  • 8 oz. good chocolate (bittersweet or semi-sweet, chopped)
  • 3/4 cup hot water
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Add flour, cocoa, soda, salt, and sugar to a bowl. Whisk.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix oil, coffee, and vanilla.
  4. Pour liquid ingredients into the dry and whisk until smooth.
  5. Add vinegar and stir. (The vinegar will react with the baking soda.)
  6. If you want a cake: oil and line the bottom of an 8" or 9" round pan. Pour in the batter.
  7. If you want cupcakes: set out 12 liners into a cupcake pan. Distribute batter evenly.
  8. For the cake: bake for 25-30 minutes. Toothpick inserted in middle should come out clean.
  9. For the cupcakes: bake for 20 minutes. Toothpick inserted in middle should come out clean.
  10. Optional glaze: Melt chocolate. (You can use a double-boiler or short microwave bursts.) Stir in hot water and vanilla until smooth. Spoon over cooled cake or cupcakes. Refrigerate until set.
